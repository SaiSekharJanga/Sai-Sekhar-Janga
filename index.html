Who We Are

Creative ambition.
Commercial acumen.

Kaiser Productions Pvt. Ltd. is a film production and financing company driven by a singular conviction: that powerful, purposeful storytelling deserves a global stage.

We operate at the intersection of creative ambition and commercial acumen — identifying, developing, and producing films that challenge, move, and resonate with audiences far beyond the multiplex.

We are not merely producers. We are collaborators, risk-takers, and champions of cinema that matters — backing bold visions from the earliest stages of a script to the final frame.

Our Philosophy
"We believe accessibility and artistic integrity are not opposing forces. The films that endure — those embraced across generations and geographies — are rooted in authentic human experience."

The Producers

Sai Sekhar Janga

Film Financier & Producer

Sai Sekhar Janga is a dynamic and forward-thinking film financier with a growing presence in the entertainment industry. He has financed films such as Badtameez Gill and Nikita Roy, along with several Malayalam and Telugu films currently in the pipeline across the South Indian film industry.

Known for his sharp creative instincts and strong understanding of storytelling, Sai has a keen eye for identifying compelling scripts and promising talent. He supports projects that balance artistic depth with commercial appeal, ensuring each venture carries both impact and integrity.

Karan Rijhwani

Producer & Creative Entrepreneur

With over eight years of experience in the film industry, Karan Rijhwani has built a reputation for his passion, dedication, and entrepreneurial spirit. Having contributed to more than seven films, he has been actively involved in creative development, post-production management, and production budgeting.

An entrepreneur at heart, he is constantly exploring new opportunities in the world of storytelling and entertainment, driven by his deep love for films and the creative process.
